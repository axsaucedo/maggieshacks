A HackDay to create revolutionary concepts to improve the life of cancer patients.

Your opportunity to make a difference.

Maggie's Hackathon is a 12-hour HackDay that aims to change the lives of cancer patients. Twenty of Britain's best developers and entrepreneurs will have a chance to hack innovative concepts together with Bloomberg developers and product designers and revolutionize the lives of cancer patients!

The inspiration for Maggie's Hack was ignited through a project ignited byMaggie's Centres, in which they envisioned

Startups, apps, prototypes will be created, and presented in front of CEOs, investors, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists - all with the objective of becoming the 2015 HackTrain champion! With TrainHackers from UK, Russia, Romania, France, Poland, Czech Republic together with many other European countries, this hackathon will certainly promise a unique experience!

Who was Maggie?

Maggie Keswick Jencks lived with advanced cancer for two years. During that time she used her knowledge and experience to create a blueprint for a new type of care. Maggie’s Centres are built around her belief that people should not “lose the joy of living in the fear of dying”.
